Our Company

RJS Construction Services, LLC Is a full service general contractor in Santa Fe, NM and surrounding area. We specialize in custom homes, Signature Homes, Restoration, Renovation, Custom Commercial, Agricultura/ Barndominum.

Ryan Skrak, owner, brings over 20 years of construction experience with projects ranging from resort club house to the highest-end custom homes. RJS Construction Services employs the finest craftsmen, skilled traders and managers in our region to service each project. Our clear communication and owner focused mission ensure that expectations are met through a team approach.

RJS Construction Services enjoys strong partnerships with architects, engineers and designers both local and around the country. These relationships help provide each project with professionalism and personal attention.
